Ask a question and click the button to get an answer




Your question:

You can ask the Magic 8 Ball the following things:



-When something will happen (include the phrase "when will" in your question)

-How you will die

-Whom you will marry

-How long something will take (include the phrase "how long" in your question)

-You can ask how the Magic 8 Ball is doing (include the phrase "how are you" in your question)

-If the Magic 8 Ball hates you (ask "Do you hate me?"). I'm not sure why you'd want to know this though.

-You can ask it for an inspirational quote (just include the word "quote" in your question)

-You can ask it to give you or a friend a fortune (just include the word "fortune" in your question)

-You can ask it a why question (just include the word "why" in your question)

-You can give it a choice between different things by typing your question or statement followed by a colon(:) and list your options separating them with the word "or".

Ex: Which band is better: Linkin Park or Imagine Dragons
Ex: Who is funnier?: Luke or Brandon or Jim or Nate

-Anything that does not fall under the above categories will be treated as a yes or no question if it has a question mark

-Anything that does not fall under the above categories and does NOT have a question mark will be treated as a statement

*There are some other things you can say to The Magic 8 Ball that you'll just have to find yourself hehehe*
